Packing Guide for April Weather

Clothing Essentials
  • Light layers (t-shirts, long sleeves)
  • Light sweater or cardigan
  • Light jacket/windbreaker
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Sandals for beach days
  • Dress shoes for dinners
  • Swimwear for beach activities
  • Light rain jacket (SF)
Business Casual
  • Collared shirts/blouses
  • Dress pants or nice jeans
  • Light blazer or cardigan
  • Dress for dinners
  • Belt and accessories
  • Professional shoes
Accessories
  • Sunglasses (essential)
  • Sun hat or cap
  • Comfortable day pack
  • Camera equipment
  • Portable phone chargers
  • Travel adapters (US plugs)
Weather Specifics
  • Sunscreen SPF 30+
  • Moisturizer (dry climate)
  • Lip balm with SPF
  • Light scarf for SF evenings
  • Umbrella (compact)
  • Reusable water bottle
Packing Tips: Pack light layers for temperature variations. California weather can change throughout the day. Comfortable walking shoes are essential for city exploration.
