Day 5: Nara Park & The Proposal

Itinerary

Morning: Take a short train ride to Nara and visit the friendly deer in Nara Park.

Lunch: Enjoy lunch in Nara before visiting Todai-ji Temple, home to a giant Buddha statue.

Afternoon: Explore the beautiful Kasuga Taisha Shrine before returning to Kyoto.
