Days 3-4: Kyoto - Temples & Culture

Day 3: Travel to Kyoto & Gion

Morning: Take the Shinkansen (bullet train) from Tokyo to Kyoto.

Afternoon: Check into your ryokan (traditional inn) and explore the historic Gion district, known for its geishas.

Evening: Enjoy a traditional kaiseki dinner at your ryokan.

Day 4: Iconic Temples

Morning: Visit Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) and the serene Ryoan-ji Temple with its famous rock garden.

Afternoon: Hike through the thousands of torii gates at Fushimi Inari Shrine.
