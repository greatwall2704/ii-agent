Days 1-2: Tokyo - Arrival & Exploration

Day 1: Arrival in Tokyo

Afternoon: Arrive at Narita (NRT) or Haneda (HND) airport. Activate your Japan Rail Pass and take the Narita Express or Limousine Bus to your hotel.

Evening: Check into your hotel and take a leisurely stroll through Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden.

Day 2: Historic & Modern Tokyo

Morning: Visit the historic Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa and browse the traditional shops on Nakamise-dori.

Afternoon: Immerse yourself in the vibrant energy of Shibuya Crossing, the world's busiest intersection.

Evening: Enjoy dinner in the lively Shibuya district.
