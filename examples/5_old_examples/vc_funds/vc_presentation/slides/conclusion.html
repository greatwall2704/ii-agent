Key Takeaways

Strategic Insights for Entrepreneurs

Market Landscape

  • Top 10 funds manage $300B+ combined AUM
  • Sequoia Capital leads with ~$85B AUM
  • General Catalyst has grown to $36B+ AUM
  • Funds are increasingly focused on AI and emerging tech
Investment Trends
• AI & Machine Learning: Universal focus area
• Global Resilience: Defense and infrastructure
• Digital Health: Post-pandemic growth
• Fintech: Continued innovation
• Climate Tech: Sustainability focus

Recommendations for Entrepreneurs

Fund Selection Strategy
1. Match your stage: Seed, Series A, or growth
2. Align with sector focus: Research fund specializations
3. Consider geography: Some funds prefer local presence
4. Evaluate value-add: Beyond capital, what support do they offer?
Approach Strategy
• Network first: Seek warm introductions
• Research thoroughly: Know their portfolio and thesis
• Be prepared: Have clear metrics and traction
• Think long-term: Choose partners, not just capital
Remember: The best VC relationships are built on mutual alignment of vision, values, and long-term goals.
