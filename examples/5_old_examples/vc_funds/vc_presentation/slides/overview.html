US Venture Capital Landscape Overview

Methodology

  • Ranked by Assets Under Management (AUM)
  • Focus on US-based funds with significant domestic presence
  • Data sourced from official websites and public disclosures
  • Includes both early-stage and growth focused funds
Note: AUM figures are approximate and based on the most recent publicly available information as of 2025.

Fund Categories

Tier 1: Mega Funds
$30B+ AUM
Examples: Sequoia Capital, Andreessen Horowitz, General Catalyst
Focus: Multi-stage investments, global reach
Tier 2: Major Funds
$15-30B AUM
Examples: Accel, NEA, Kleiner Perkins
Focus: Established players with strong track records
Tier 3: Established Funds
$5-15B AUM
Examples: Benchmark, Greylock, Lightspeed
Focus: Specialized expertise and sector focus
