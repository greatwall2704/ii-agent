Trip Overview

This 7-day itinerary is designed for a romantic and culturally immersive trip to Japan for two. It balances iconic landmarks with hidden gems, offering a blend of history, nature, and unique cultural experiences. The budget for this trip is estimated to be between $2500 and $5000, covering flights, accommodation, activities, and transportation.

  • Duration: 7 Days / 8 Nights
  • Budget: $2500 - $5000
  • Focus: History, Culture, Romance, Hidden Gems
  • Destinations: Tokyo, Nara, Kyoto
