Day 6: The Proposal & Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

Today is the day! Head to the beautiful Arashiyama district. Take a romantic boat ride on the Hozugawa River, surrounded by stunning scenery. In the afternoon, find a secluded spot in the magical Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, a truly unforgettable setting for a proposal. Celebrate this special moment with a romantic dinner at a restaurant overlooking the river.
