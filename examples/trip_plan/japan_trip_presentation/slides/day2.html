Day 2: Tokyo's History and Culture

Today, you'll delve into Tokyo's rich history and culture. Start your day with a visit to the iconic Senso-ji Temple, Tokyo's oldest temple. Afterward, explore the bustling Nakamise-dori street leading to the temple, filled with traditional snacks and souvenirs. In the afternoon, immerse yourselves in the art of the Japanese tea ceremony, a serene and beautiful cultural experience.
