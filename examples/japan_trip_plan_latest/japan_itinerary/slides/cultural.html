Cultural Experiences Summary

Kendo Experience

Location: Traditional dojo in Tokyo

Duration: 2 hours

Cost: ~$40 per person

  • Learn basic sword techniques
  • Understand samurai philosophy
  • Experience Japanese discipline
  • Wear traditional hakama

Tea Ceremony

Location: Traditional tea house, Kyoto

Duration: 2 hours

Cost: ~$35 per person

  • Learn the Way of Tea (Chado)
  • Practice mindfulness and presence
  • Understand Japanese aesthetics
  • Taste authentic matcha

Zen Meditation

Location: Shunko-in Temple, Kyoto

Duration: 1 hour

Cost: ~$25 per person

  • Guided zazen meditation
  • English-speaking monk instruction
  • Temple garden contemplation
  • Buddhist philosophy introduction

Kaiseki Dining

Location: Traditional restaurants

Frequency: 2 dinners

Cost: ~$80-120 per person

  • Multi-course seasonal cuisine
  • Artistic food presentation
  • Traditional dining etiquette
  • Local sake pairing

Total Cultural Investment

Approximately $300-400 for both - invaluable cultural immersion and lifelong memories
