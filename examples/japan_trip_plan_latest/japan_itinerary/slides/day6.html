Day 6: Nara - Deer Park & Special Proposal Location

Nara Itinerary

Morning: Train to Nara (45 minutes from Kyoto)

10:00: Nara Park - feed the sacred deer

12:00: Lunch at traditional restaurant

13:30: Todai-ji Temple (Great Buddha)

15:00: Kasuga Taisha Shrine

17:00: Return to Kyoto

Nara Highlights

• 1,200+ free-roaming deer

• Largest bronze Buddha statue

• 3,000 stone lanterns at Kasuga

• Japan's first permanent capital

SPECIAL PROPOSAL LOCATIONS

1. Kamo River Banks (Kyoto)

Romantic riverside setting with evening lights reflecting on water. Free, peaceful, and quintessentially Kyoto.

2. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

Mystical bamboo forest creating natural cathedral. Quiet in early evening, magical lighting.

3. Kiyomizu-dera Wooden Stage

Iconic temple with panoramic city views. Symbolic of taking a "leap of faith" together.

Recommended: Kamo River at sunset

Proposal Tip: Bring a small picnic blanket, arrive 30 minutes before sunset, and have a backup indoor location ready in case of rain.
