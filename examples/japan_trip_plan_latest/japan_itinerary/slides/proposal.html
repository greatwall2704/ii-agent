Proposal Location Recommendations

Kamo River Banks

Kyoto

Pros:
  • Romantic riverside setting
  • Beautiful evening lighting
  • Free and accessible
  • Quintessentially Kyoto
Cons:
  • Weather dependent
  • Some foot traffic

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

Kyoto

Pros:
  • Mystical atmosphere
  • Natural cathedral effect
  • Unique photo opportunity
  • Quiet in early evening
Cons:
  • Can be crowded
  • Limited seating

Tokyo Skytree

Tokyo

Pros:
  • Panoramic city views
  • Weather protected
  • Memorable backdrop
  • Professional photos available
Cons:
  • Expensive (~$25 entry)
  • Very public
  • Tourist crowds

Our Top Recommendation: Kamo River at Sunset

The perfect blend of romance, authenticity, and Japanese beauty. Arrive 30 minutes before sunset for the best lighting and atmosphere.

Proposal Success Tips

Timing: Golden hour (1 hour before sunset) for best lighting

Backup Plan: Have an indoor location ready for rainy weather

Photography: Consider hiring a local photographer for the moment

Ring Security: Keep ring in hotel safe until the day

Language: Learn "Kekkon shite kudasai" (Will you marry me?)

Celebration: Book a special dinner reservation for after!
