Equities: Where to Focus

Quality & Resilience

  • Banks with prudent underwriting, strong capitalization, and fee income
  • Consumer staples and utilities for defensiveness
  • Industrials & logistics tied to FDI and infrastructure buildout
  • Selective tech/services; exporters with pricing power and diversified customers

Diversify via broad ETFs and avoid over-concentration: single name <5% , sector <15% of total.

Position for domestic demand and FDI-led growth.
