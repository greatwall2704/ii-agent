How Tariffs Hit Portfolios

Demand & Margins

Export-heavy sectors may see margin compression unless value-add and pricing power offset higher costs.

USD & Financial Conditions

Tariff shocks often strengthen the USD and tighten global conditions—raising EM FX volatility.

Supply Chains (China+1)

Vietnam benefits from FDI relocation, contingent on rules-of-origin compliance and local value-add.

Commodities & Inflation

Import prices and supply frictions can lift CPI; watch oil and metals for pass-through.
