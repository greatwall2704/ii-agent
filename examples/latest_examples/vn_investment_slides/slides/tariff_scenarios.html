Tariff Backdrop & Scenarios

Scenario A

Broad base tariff plus elevated China-specific rates. Stronger USD, slower trade; Vietnam benefits from China+1 FDI but faces demand headwinds.

Scenario B

Targeted sector tariffs and tighter anti-circumvention. Sector-specific shocks with continued selective FDI into Vietnam.

Scenario C

Limited changes, gradualism, and negotiations. Cyclical drivers dominate; constructive for Vietnam risk assets.

Portfolio Implications

  • Maintain USD sleeve to hedge VND in stress
  • Use short-duration VND bonds as ballast
  • Diversify Vietnam equities toward quality and domestic demand
