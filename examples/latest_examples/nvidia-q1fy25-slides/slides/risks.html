Risks & Watch Items

Execution & Supply

Sustaining supply for next-gen platforms while managing long lead-time components and networking capacity remains critical. Any disruption could extend delivery timelines and impact mix.

Competition

Rivals include AMD’s latest accelerators and custom silicon from hyperscalers. Relative performance, software ecosystem depth, and networking differentiation are key battlegrounds.

Regulatory / Geo

Export controls and geopolitics could constrain sales into certain regions or require product variants, affecting margin and allocation.

Demand Elasticity

Enterprise digestion phases vs. new workloads and sovereign AI initiatives create timing variability, though secular AI drivers remain strong.
