Platforms & Networking

Compute + Network = Throughput

NVIDIA’s platform strategy couples successive GPU architectures with purpose-built interconnects, systems, and software to maximize end-to-end AI throughput, lowering total cost and time-to-train/infer.

  • Architecture cadence: Hopper → Blackwell with trillion-parameter scale
  • Switches: Quantum (InfiniBand) and Spectrum-X (Ethernet X800)
  • Systems: DGX SuperPOD; reference designs for AI factories
  • Cloud partners: AWS, Google Cloud, Microsoft, Oracle expand GenAI services

Source: NVIDIA press release and product announcements (Q1 FY2025).

Networking is a strategic lever: bandwidth and low-latency fabrics drive cluster efficiency.
