Mix and Scale Expand Margins

Gross margin expanded to 78.4% GAAP (78.9% non-GAAP), up 2.4/2.2 points Q/Q, as mix shifted to high-value Data Center platforms and software, and operating leverage drove outsized operating income growth.

  • GAAP GM: 78.4% | Non-GAAP GM: 78.9%
  • Operating expenses: $3.50B GAAP (+10% Q/Q)
  • Operating income: $16.9B GAAP (+24% Q/Q)
  • Non-GAAP operating income: $18.1B

Source: NVIDIA Q1 FY2025 press release tables.
