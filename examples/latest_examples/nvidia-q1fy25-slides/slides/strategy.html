Strategy & Catalysts

Platform Cadence & Software

NVIDIA’s strategy centers on rapid architecture cadence (Hopper → Blackwell) combined with end-to-end systems, networking fabrics, and a growing software stack (AI Enterprise, NIM) that increases value capture and stickiness.

  • AI factories & sovereign AI: accelerating capex cycles
  • Blackwell in full production: trillion-parameter scale readiness
  • Software attach: monetizing deployment, management, and inference
  • Verticals: healthcare, automotive, robotics expanding TAM

Source: NVIDIA Q1 FY2025 press release; product announcements.

Software and platform cadence underpin durable pricing power and margins.
