Quarter at a Glance — Q1 FY2025

Headline KPIs

NVIDIA delivered record results as accelerated computing demand surged across training and inference workloads. Growth was led by Data Center, with broad-based customer adoption and continued mix shift toward high-value platforms and software.

  • Revenue: $26.0B (+18% Q/Q, +262% Y/Y)
  • Data Center revenue: $22.6B (+23% Q/Q, +427% Y/Y)
  • GAAP EPS: $5.98 ; Non-GAAP EPS: $6.12
  • Gross Margin: 78.4% GAAP | 78.9% non-GAAP
  • Operating income: $16.9B ; Free Cash Flow: $14.9B
  • Capital return: $7.74B buybacks; dividend raised 150% (post-split $0.01/share)

Source: NVIDIA Newsroom, “NVIDIA Announces Financial Results for First Quarter Fiscal 2025,” May 22, 2024.
