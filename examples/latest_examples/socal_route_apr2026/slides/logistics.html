Logistics — Transport, Hotels, Timing

Vehicles

Two 8-passenger vans offer flexibility for split activities. Alternative: one private minibus with driver for simpler parking and alcohol-included days.

Drive Times (no traffic)

  • LAX → Malibu: 45–60 min
  • LA → Laguna Beach: 1h15
  • Laguna → Temecula: 1h15
  • Temecula → San Diego: 1h
  • San Diego → LAX: 2h30

Hotel Zones

LA Westside/Santa Monica; Laguna oceanfront; Temecula resort; San Diego Bayfront (walkable to dining).

Packing Tips

  • Layers and light windbreakers for cool evenings.
  • Comfortable walking shoes and sun protection (hat, SPF 30+).
  • Compact backpacks for short trails and museum days.
