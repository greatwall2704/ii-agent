Flights & Arrival Strategy

Paris → LAX

Recommend departing CDG on Mon Apr 20 evening to land at LAX midday Tue Apr 21. Nonstop options run ~11 hours. Allow 2–3 hours for entry, bags, and vehicle pickup.

  • Time zone: PDT (UTC−7).
  • Ground: 2× 8-passenger vans at LAX; backup: private minibus with driver.
  • First night hotel zone: Westside LA or Santa Monica.

Light first day: Santa Monica Pier and Promenade; early dinner; early night to reset jet lag.

Overnight flight recommended to arrive rested for a coastal evening.
