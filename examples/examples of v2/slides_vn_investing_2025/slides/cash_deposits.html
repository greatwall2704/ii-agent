Cash & Term Deposits (VND)

Use a liquidity ladder to preserve optionality and earn carry.

Recommended Ladder

Split cash across tenors to blend yield and access:

  • 30% in 1–3 months (emergency + tactical dry powder)
  • 30% in 6 months (core buffer)
  • 40% in 12 months (carry capture)

Focus on top-tier banks; diversify across 2–3 institutions. Reinvest every month to smooth rates.

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Capital safety, predictable income, flexibility to redeploy.
  • Cons: After-tax real return can be modest if inflation surprises.

As of May 2025, 6–12m deposit rates ~4.5–5.5% (SBV).
