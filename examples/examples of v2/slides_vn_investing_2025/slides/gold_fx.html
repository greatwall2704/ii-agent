Gold and USD Exposure: Hedging Playbook

Use measured hedges against inflation and VND weakness.

Gold

  • Target 6–12% allocation depending on risk profile.
  • Use SJC-eligible local products or global ETFs via offshore broker.
  • Buy in tranches; avoid chasing local premium spikes.

USD

  • Hold 8–15% in USD cash/deposits as FX buffer.
  • Convert opportunistically on VND strength; avoid peak-stress conversions.
  • Regulatory note: USD deposit interest is typically 0% in Vietnam; consider short-dated T-Bills via offshore.

Hedges work best when sized modestly and bought gradually.
