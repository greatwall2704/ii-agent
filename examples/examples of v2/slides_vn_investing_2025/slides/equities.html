Vietnam Equities: Sectors and Tilts

Favor domestic-demand and supply-chain winners; stay selective on US-exposed exporters.

Attractive Tilts

  • Retail banks with strong CASA and fee income
  • Logistics/ports, industrial parks, warehousing
  • Power/energy and telecom infrastructure
  • FMCG and discretionary with pricing power

Use These Vehicles

  • VN30 ETFs for core exposure
  • Thematic ETFs/funds for logistics/industrial
  • Direct blue-chips with consistent governance

Caution List

  • Exporters to the US without tariff pass-through
  • Highly leveraged developers
  • Small caps with thin liquidity
