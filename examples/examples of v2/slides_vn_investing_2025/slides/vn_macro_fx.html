Vietnam Macro & FX: 2025 Snapshot

Growth resilient, inflation within 4.5–5.0% target band; SBV accommodative; VND softer vs. USD.

Data Points

  • GDP growth forecasts: ~6.5–7.0% for 2025 (UOB 6.9%, Jul 2025).
  • Inflation: Government tolerance 4.5–5.0% (Standard Chartered via VietnamPlus, Mar 2025).
  • Deposit rates (SBV, May 2025): 6–12m tenor ~4.5–5.5% p.a.; 12–24m ~4.8–6.0% p.a.; 24m+ ~6.9–7.1% p.a.
  • USD/VND traded near 26,200–26,450 in Aug 2025; 12m drift risk upward if US rates stay high.

Implications

  • Carry on VND deposits attractive vs. inflation expectations if laddered.
  • Some USD cash acts as FX shock absorber amid tariff uncertainty.
  • Equities benefit from domestic-demand themes; exporters mixed.

Macro tailwinds with manageable FX risk favor a barbell of deposits/bonds and selective equities.
