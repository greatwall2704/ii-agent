Tariffs 2025: What Changed and Why It Matters

US universal tariff baseline with higher tiers for deficit countries; Vietnam listed at 20% in late-July plans.

Key Facts

  • Baseline 10% tariff; 15% for deficit countries; 20%+ for 26 countries incl. Vietnam (CNN, Jul 31, 2025).
  • Implementation dates and legal challenges create path dependency and volatility.
  • EU, JP, KR negotiated down to ~15% on most goods; CN framework pending extension.

Transmission to Vietnam

  • Export price pressure for US-destined goods (apparel, furniture, electronics components).
  • Re-shoring/diversion benefits: ASEAN as alternative to CN can boost FDI into VN logistics/industrial parks.
  • Imported inflation and supply-chain friction likely to weigh on margins near term.

Global trade re-wiring: tariffs pressure exporters but can re-route FDI to Vietnam.
