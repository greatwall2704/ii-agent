Government & Corporate Bonds

Income foundation with selective credit exposure.

VGB Strategy

  • Focus on 2–5 year VGBs for yield-duration balance; avoid long duration in case of FX/inflation shocks.
  • Use staggered purchases (DCA) to manage rate volatility.

Corporate Bond Filters

  • Audited statements; interest coverage >3x; net leverage <2.5x.
  • Avoid opaque real estate issuers; prefer utilities, SOE-linked, logistics with stable cash flows.
  • Check custody and liquidity; buy exchange-listed where possible.

Keep credit quality high; use VGBs as ballast.
