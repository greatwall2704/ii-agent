Flow Matching: Core Idea

Objective

Learn a time-dependent vector field v_θ(x,t) whose flow transports a simple base p0 to the data distribution p1.

  • Dynamics: ∂ t x = v θ (x,t)
  • Target conditional velocity: u t (x t )
  • Loss: E t,x t [||v θ (x t ,t) − u t (x t )||²]

Intuition

  • Instead of predicting noise or score, predict velocity.
  • Train with supervised MSE against analytically derived u t .
  • Sampling is integrating the learned ODE from noise to data.
