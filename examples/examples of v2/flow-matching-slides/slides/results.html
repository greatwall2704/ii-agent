Empirical Results

Quality vs Steps

  • RF/FM achieve competitive FID with 10–30 ODE steps.
  • Diffusion baselines often need 50–100+ steps.
  • Straight paths lower stiffness → faster sampling.

Observations

  • Schedules, backbones, and guidance strongly matter.
  • Too few steps can oversmooth; moderate CFG helps.
  • Latent-space RF/FM scales better to high resolutions.
