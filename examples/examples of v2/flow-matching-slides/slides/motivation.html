Why Flow Matching?

Efficient training and sampling by learning a velocity field that transports noise to data.

Limitations of Diffusion

  • Great image quality but often 20–100+ sampling steps.
  • Score estimation requires denoising objectives and SNR weighting.
  • Stiff ODE/SDE dynamics near t≈0 or t≈1.

Advantages of Flow Matching

  • Directly learns a time-dependent vector field v(x,t).
  • Simpler MSE objective against closed-form target velocities.
  • Fewer ODE steps possible with straightened trajectories.
  • Flexible conditioning (text/class) and guidance.
