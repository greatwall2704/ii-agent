Tips and Pitfalls

Common Issues

  • Boundary mismatches at t≈0 or t≈1.
  • Exploding velocities when schedules are too aggressive.
  • Crossing trajectories in non-rectified paths.

Practical Tips

  • Visualize 2D toy vector fields early.
  • Clip gradients; use EMA and loss smoothing.
  • Start with linear/rectified interpolants; then add noise.
