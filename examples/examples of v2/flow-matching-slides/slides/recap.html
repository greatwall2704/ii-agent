Recap: Diffusions and Probability Flow ODE

SDE/ODE Views

  • Forward SDE corrupts data to noise; reverse SDE denoises.
  • Probability flow ODE shares marginals with the SDE but is deterministic.
  • Sampling via standard ODE solvers (Euler, Heun, RK4).

Link to CNFs

  • Continuous normalizing flows parameterize the vector field.
  • FM uses supervised targets for the vector field instead of log-likelihood gradients.
  • Bridges diffusion objectives and CNF training.
