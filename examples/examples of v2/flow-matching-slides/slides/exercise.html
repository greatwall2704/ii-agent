In-Class Exercise

Task

Implement flow matching on a 2D Gaussian mixture.

  • Define x t =(1−t)x 0 +t x 1 with small noise.
  • Train a small MLP to predict velocity u t .
  • Integrate ODE from x 0 to generate samples.

Deliverables

  • Plots: vector field, trajectories, and final samples.
  • Compare linear vs rectified paths.
  • Short report on step-efficiency and stability.
