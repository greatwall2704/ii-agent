Architectures for FM/RF

Backbones

  • U-Net: proven for pixel-space models; multi-scale context.
  • DiT (Diffusion Transformer): strong scaling, patch tokens.
  • Latent models: VAE/VQ encoders for high-res images.

Conditioning

  • Text encoders (T5/CLIP), class labels, or layout maps.
  • Time/step embeddings (Fourier, RoPE) for t-awareness.
  • Predict velocity directly; optionally rescale targets.
