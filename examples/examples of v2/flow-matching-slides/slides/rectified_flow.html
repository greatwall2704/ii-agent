Rectified Flow (RF)

Straightening Trajectories

  • Use straight paths: x t =(1−t)x 0 +t x 1 .
  • Target velocity roughly constant along the path.
  • Reduces path crossings and stiffness.

Practice

  • Often enables high quality in ~10–30 ODE steps.
  • Compatible with DiT backbones and CFG-like guidance.
  • Strong empirical performance on images and video.
