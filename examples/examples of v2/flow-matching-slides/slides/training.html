Training Details

Objective & Weighting

  • Sample t∼ρ(t); compute x t and u t .
  • Loss = w(t) · ||v θ (x t ,t) − u t ||².
  • Choose ρ, w to focus on hard time regions.

Practicalities

  • Base p 0 : Gaussian or learned prior.
  • Batch endpoint pairs; cache text embeddings.
  • Use AMP, gradient clipping, EMA; monitor boundary errors.
