Notes & Data Caveats

Comparability

AUM figures can reflect different concepts: regulatory assets under management reported in Form ADV, committed capital across venture and growth vehicles, or total firm assets including cross-over funds. Where possible, we used conservative ranges and will reconcile to SEC ADV per firm.

Contacts

Direct emails are often not published by top-tier VC firms. We provide firmwide contact emails or contact forms. If you prefer targeted partner emails, we can assemble a role-based directory in a separate deliverable.
