Methodology & Scope

U.S.-based venture capital firms ranked by estimated Assets Under Management (AUM). AUM expressed as ranges based on public firm disclosures and reputable secondary sources; will be refreshed with SEC ADV figures upon access.

Inclusions

  • Dedicated venture/growth equity franchises headquartered in the U.S.
  • Where applicable, growth and late-stage vehicles included.
  • AUM figures normalized to USD; ranges reflect uncertainty and timing.

Sources & Contacts

  • Firm websites, press releases, reputable media and investor materials.
  • Best-available outreach email or contact form is provided for each firm.
  • Planned second pass to pin to SEC Form ADV where available.
