Asset Class Review from Vietnam

VND Cash/Deposits

Ladder maturities (instant access + 3–12 months). Use reputable banks and mind insurer limits.

VND Bonds

Government/high-grade corporate, 1–5y duration for core income.

Vietnam Equities

Tilt to re‑shoring plays: logistics, industrial parks, select exporters; balance with defensives.

Global/USD

USD T‑bills/IG bond ETFs; modest global equity ETF sleeve for diversification.

Gold & Optional Crypto

Gold 5–10% as hedge; crypto only low single-digits if comfortable.

Real Estate

Prefer cash‑flowing assets or listed vehicles; size prudently due to illiquidity.
