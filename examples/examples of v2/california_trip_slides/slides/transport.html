Parking & Toll Tips

Street meters accept cards/apps; beach lots are easiest for groups. California has few toll roads on this route; mind FasTrak lanes around Orange County. Consider EV rentals only if hotels provide overnight charging.

Navigation: Use offline maps and set “Avoid tolls” to prevent surprise FasTrak charges.
