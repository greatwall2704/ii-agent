Important Notes

  • Japan Rail Pass: A 7-day Japan Rail Pass is recommended for this itinerary. It will cover your travel between Tokyo and Kyoto, as well as the day trip to Nara.
  • Packing: Pack comfortable walking shoes, as you'll be doing a lot of exploring on foot. Also, pack layers of clothing, as the weather in April can be unpredictable.
  • Cash: While credit cards are widely accepted in major cities, it's always a good idea to have some cash on hand for smaller shops and restaurants.
  • Connectivity: Consider renting a pocket Wi-Fi device or purchasing a Japanese SIM card to stay connected during your trip.
