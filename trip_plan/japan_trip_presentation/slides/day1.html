Day 1: Arrival in Tokyo

Welcome to Japan! Upon arrival at Narita (NRT) or Haneda (HND) airport, you'll take the Narita Express or Limousine Bus to your hotel in Shinjuku. After settling in, take a leisurely stroll to the nearby Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden, a beautiful oasis in the heart of the city. Enjoy a romantic evening exploring the vibrant Shinjuku district and savor your first authentic Japanese dinner.
